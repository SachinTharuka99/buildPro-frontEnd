Connecting Clients with Skilled Construction Freelancers

BuildPro Solutions is an online platform designed to connect clients with qualified freelancers across the construction industry. Whether you need plumbing, electrical work, or masonry services, our platform simplifies the process by matching your job requests with skilled professionals.

Traditional hiring methods are slow and unreliable. With BuildPro, clients can post jobs, receive competitive bids, and select the best expert based on reviews and ratings. Freelancers benefit by showcasing their skills, building a solid reputation, and finding consistent work.

Need Help? Explore Our FAQs

Find answers to common questions about using BuildPro — whether you're a client or a freelancer.

How do I post a job request on BuildPro?

Log in to your BuildPro account, go to the dashboard, and click on "Post a Job". Fill in the service type, location, and project details, then publish it for freelancers to view and bid.

How do freelancers get hired?

Freelancers can apply to job requests or be contacted directly through their profile. Once hired, project milestones and communication are managed within the BuildPro platform.

Is there a verification process for service providers?

Yes, all freelancers must submit valid identification and portfolio evidence for verification. Verified accounts are labeled for client trust.

How is payment handled on BuildPro?

Clients fund the project upon agreement. BuildPro holds the payment in escrow and releases it when both parties confirm milestone completion or full delivery.

What happens in case of disputes?

BuildPro provides a dispute resolution system. If any issue arises, both parties can submit evidence, and our support team will mediate the situation fairly.

Can I subscribe to a freelancer’s services?

Yes, BuildPro offers subscription options for recurring services. Visit the freelancer's profile and choose the subscription plan that suits your needs.
